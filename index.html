Rules

Here are the rules for the Clash of Clans Clan War League:

  • War preference should be set to green to participate in CWL.
  • Must follow clan emails.
  • No 1-star attacks allowed, always aim for a minimum of two stars.
  • No missed attacks allowed. If you miss one round, you won't participate in the remaining rounds. Chances of demotion if you are an elder or co-leader.
  • Inform early in clan chat if you are not available for any round.
